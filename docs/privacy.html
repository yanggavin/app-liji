Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 10, 2025

Introduction

Liji (禮記) ("the App") is developed by Gavin Yang ("we", "us", "our"). We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding your information.

Data We Collect

DataSourcePurpose
Person namesYour inputIdentify gift recipients/senders
Transaction detailsYour inputRecord amounts, occasions, dates, notes
Relationship tagsYour inputCategorise contacts (Family, Friend, etc.)
Contact identifierContacts (optional)Link to your address book for quick lookup
Purchase statusApp StoreDetermine ad-free entitlement

Data Storage

All your personal data (names, transactions, tags) is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. If you enable iCloud Sync, your data is also stored in your private iCloud account via Apple's CloudKit. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

We do not operate any external servers or databases. Your data never leaves your device or iCloud account.

Contacts Access

The App may request access to your Contacts to help you quickly find people when recording gifts. This access is entirely optional. Contact data is only used locally for name lookup and is never transmitted externally.

Advertising

The free (international) version of Liji displays banner ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve ads. For details, see Google's Privacy Policy.

We use Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework to request your permission before any tracking occurs. You can deny tracking and the App will continue to function normally with non-personalised ads.

The paid version (China App Store) contains no advertising and does not request tracking permission.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information. Purchase status (ad-free entitlement) is verified via Apple's StoreKit framework.

Analytics

We do not use any third-party analytics or crash reporting SDKs. We do not collect usage analytics, device information, or crash logs.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device and in your private iCloud account, you have full control:

  • Delete — Delete any record, person, or tag within the App, or delete the App to remove all local data.
  • Export — Use the CSV export feature to download a copy of your data.
  • iCloud — Manage or delete synced data through your iCloud settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@liji-app.newtree.tech.
